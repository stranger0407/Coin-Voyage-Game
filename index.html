Coin-Voyage

How to Play?

Objective:

  • Collect as many coins as possible! The player with the highest score at the end wins. Game Rules:
  • Players take turns moving across the grid. - Move up (↑), down (↓), left (←), or right (→) to collect coins.
  • Coins have different values:

    1 Point

    3 Points

    5 Points

    The game ends when all coins are collected.
  • The player with the highest score wins!
